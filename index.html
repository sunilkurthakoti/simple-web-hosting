About the Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Role: Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates

Abilities: Possesses the power of the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Gum-Gum Fruit), making his body rubber-like.

Goal: To find the legendary treasure, *One Piece*, and become the Pirate King.

Luffy is known for his cheerful personality, relentless spirit, and belief in his friends. His dream fuels the journey that inspires his entire crew.

Roronoa Zoro

Role: Swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates

Abilities: Master of the Three-Sword Style, capable of incredible feats of strength and endurance.

Goal: To become the world's greatest swordsman.

Zoro is a loyal and disciplined fighter who values strength and honor. His ambition and dedication to his craft make him a key member of the crew.
